# Setup Telegram Bot

1.  **Open Telegram**: Open the Telegram app on your phone or desktop.
2.  **Find BotFather**: Search for the user `@BotFather` (verified account).
3.  **Start Chat**: Click "Start" or type `/start`.
4.  **Create New Bot**: Type `/newbot` and follow the instructions:
    *   Enter a **name** for your bot (e.g., "My Wishing Bot").
    *   Enter a **username** (must end in `bot`, e.g., `my_wishing_tool_bot`).
5.  **Get Token**: BotFather will give you an **API Token**. It looks like `123456789:ABCdefGhIjkLmnOpQrStUvWxYz`.
    *   **Keep this secure!**
6.  **Get Your Chat ID** (for testing):
    *   Search for `@userinfobot`.
    *   Click "Start".
    *   It will reply with your `Id`. This is your `CHAT_ID`.
